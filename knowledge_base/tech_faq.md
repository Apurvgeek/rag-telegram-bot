# Tech FAQ — Wireless Headphones (Model XB-500)

## Setup & Pairing
**How do I pair the XB-500 for the first time?**
Hold the power button for 5 seconds until the LED flashes blue and white.
Open Bluetooth settings on your device and select "XB-500" from the list.
The LED will turn solid blue once connected.

**Can I pair with multiple devices?**
Yes. The XB-500 supports multipoint pairing with up to 2 devices simultaneously.
To add a second device, put the headphones into pairing mode while already connected to the first device.

## Battery & Charging
**How long does the battery last?**
Up to 30 hours of playback at 50% volume with ANC disabled.
Active Noise Cancellation reduces battery life to approximately 22 hours.

**How long does a full charge take?**
2.5 hours via USB-C. A 15-minute quick charge provides 3 hours of playback.

## Troubleshooting
**No sound from left earcup.**
1. Disconnect and reconnect the Bluetooth connection.
2. Clean the 3.5mm jack with compressed air.
3. Perform a factory reset: hold both the volume-down and power buttons for 10 seconds.

**Microphone not working during calls.**
Ensure the call app has microphone permissions.
Check that the headphones are set as the audio input device in system sound settings.
