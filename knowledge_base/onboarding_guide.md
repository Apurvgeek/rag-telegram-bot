# Company Onboarding Guide

## Your First Day
- Arrive at 9 AM at the main reception. Bring a government-issued photo ID.
- You will be greeted by your buddy (assigned the week before joining).
- IT will set up your laptop, email, and Slack access in the morning session.

## Tools We Use
| Tool       | Purpose                        | Access via          |
|------------|--------------------------------|---------------------|
| Slack      | Team communication             | Invite sent to email |
| Jira       | Sprint planning & bug tracking | SSO with Google     |
| Confluence | Documentation & wikis          | SSO with Google     |
| GitHub     | Source code                    | IT request ticket   |
| Figma      | Design assets                  | Design team invite  |

## HR & Payroll
- Payroll runs on the 28th of every month. First payslip may be prorated.
- Submit timesheets in Workday by end-of-day Friday each week.
- For leave requests, use the "Time Off" module in Workday. Manager approval required for > 3 consecutive days.

## Benefits
- Health insurance begins on day one. Enrol within 30 days on the BenefitHub portal.
- Annual learning budget: $1,500 for courses, certifications, or conferences.
- 20 days PTO per year (prorated in year one), plus 10 public holidays.

## Probation Period
The standard probation period is 90 days. A check-in meeting occurs at 30 and 60 days.
Confirmation of employment is sent via email at day 91 if no concerns are raised.
