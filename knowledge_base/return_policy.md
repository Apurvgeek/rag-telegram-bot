# Return & Refund Policy

## Standard Return Window
Customers may return any unused item within 30 days of purchase for a full refund.
Items must be in their original packaging and include the receipt.

## Exceptions
- Digital products and software licenses are non-refundable once activated.
- Perishable goods (food, flowers) cannot be returned.
- Sale items marked "Final Sale" are not eligible for returns.

## How to Initiate a Return
1. Log in to your account and navigate to *Order History*.
2. Select the item and click *Request Return*.
3. Print the prepaid shipping label emailed to you.
4. Drop the package at any authorised carrier location.

Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days of receiving the item.

## Exchanges
If you received a defective or wrong item, contact support within 7 days.
We will send a replacement at no extra cost.
